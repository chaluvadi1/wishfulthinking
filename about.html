About this place

A blog for ideas worth sitting with.

wieshfulthinking is where I write about the things I can't stop turning over in my mind — new technology that changes how I see the world, ideas that feel underexplored, futures that seem closer than they look.

I write because committing a thought to the page is the only real way I know to find out if I actually believe it. Everything here goes through that test. If it survives, it gets published.

No schedule, no niche, no algorithm to please. Just honest thinking, written as clearly as I can manage.
